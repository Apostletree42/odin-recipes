Paneer Masala

Description

Paneer Masala is a popular North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a rich and flavorful tomato-based gravy. It's a creamy and delicious dish often served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian Cottage Cheese)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Green Chilies
  • Cashew Nuts
  • Yogurt
  • Cream
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Coriander Powder
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves
  • Oil or Ghee
  • Salt

Steps

Step 1: Preparation

  1. Cut paneer into cubes and soak them in warm water to keep them soft.
  2. Grind onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and cashew nuts into a smooth paste.

Step 2: Cooking

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Pour the ground paste into the pan. Cook until the mixture thickens and the oil separates.
  4. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for a few more minutes.
  5. Whisk yogurt and add it to the gravy. Cook until the gravy thickens.
  6. Add paneer cubes and mix gently to coat them with the gravy.
  7. Drizzle cream and sprinkle garam masala. Cook for a couple of minutes.

Step 3: Garnish and Serve

  1. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  2. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
